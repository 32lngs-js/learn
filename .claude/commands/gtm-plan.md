# Go-to-Market Plan Builder

Create a structured GTM plan for launching to a specific segment or launching a specific course/feature.

## Inputs

- **What are we launching?** (the whole platform, a specific course, a feature like AI interviews, enterprise offering)
- **Target segment** (career switchers, CFA candidates, developers, poker players, teams/enterprise)
- **Budget range** (bootstrap/zero, small < $1k/mo, medium < $5k/mo, growth > $5k/mo)
- **Timeline** (when do we want traction? 30 days, 90 days, 6 months)
- **Existing assets** (email list, social following, partnerships, domain authority)

If the user doesn't provide these, ask before proceeding.

## Process

### Phase 1 — Segment Deep-Dive

Define the target segment:
- **Size estimate** (TAM, SAM, SOM)
- **Where they are** (online communities, social platforms, search behavior)
- **Current alternatives** they use
- **Decision triggers** (what makes them try something new?)
- **Willingness to pay** signals

Use web search to find current data on segment size and behavior.

### Phase 2 — Channel Strategy

For each channel, assess fit and prioritize:

**Organic (low cost, slow)**
- SEO / content marketing — keyword opportunities, content types
- Social media — which platforms, content format, posting cadence
- Community — Reddit, Discord, forums, Hacker News
- Product-led growth — viral loops, referrals, public profiles

**Paid (fast, costs money)**
- Search ads (Google) — keyword targets, estimated CPC
- Social ads (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Meta) — targeting options
- Sponsorships — newsletters, podcasts, YouTube channels relevant to segment

**Partnerships (medium speed, relationship-dependent)**
- Influencer/creator collaborations
- University/bootcamp partnerships
- Corporate L&D partnerships
- Cross-promotions with complementary tools

Recommend a prioritized channel mix based on budget and timeline.

### Phase 3 — Messaging & Creative

For the top 3 channels, draft:
- **Hook / headline** (what stops the scroll?)
- **Value prop** (why should they care?)
- **Social proof** (what builds trust?)
- **CTA** (what's the next step?)
- **Landing page structure** (hero, benefits, social proof, CTA)

### Phase 4 — Funnel & Conversion

Map the conversion funnel:
- **Awareness** → how do they discover us?
- **Consideration** → what convinces them to try?
- **Activation** → what's the "aha moment" in first session?
- **Retention** → what brings them back daily?
- **Revenue** → what triggers upgrade to paid?
- **Referral** → what makes them share?

For each stage, define the key metric and a target.

### Phase 5 — Experiment Plan

Design 3 concrete experiments to validate the GTM:
- **Experiment name**
- **Hypothesis**
- **Setup** (what to build/run)
- **Duration** (how long to run)
- **Primary KPI** and success threshold
- **Cost estimate**

### Phase 6 — Timeline & Milestones

Create a week-by-week plan:
- Week 1-2: Setup (landing pages, tracking, content)
- Week 3-4: Launch experiments
- Week 5-8: Measure, iterate
- Week 9-12: Double down on winners

Present the complete GTM plan as an executable brief with clear owners and deadlines.
