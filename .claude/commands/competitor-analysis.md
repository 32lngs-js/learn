# Competitor Analysis

Perform a structured competitive analysis to identify differentiation opportunities.

## Inputs

- **Competitor(s) to analyze** (e.g., Brilliant, Duolingo, Codecademy, Coursera, DataCamp, etc.)
- **Focus area** (overall strategy, pricing, UX, content, gamification, AI features, enterprise)
- **Your product's current positioning** (if available — run /positioning first)

If the user just names a competitor, analyze them comprehensively.

## Process

### Phase 1 — Company Profile

Research and document:
- **Founded / HQ / Funding** (stage, total raised, last round)
- **Mission statement** and public positioning
- **User base** (reported numbers, growth trajectory)
- **Revenue model** (freemium, subscription, enterprise, certificates)
- **Key metrics** (if public — ARR, users, NPS, app ratings)

### Phase 2 — Product Analysis

Analyze their product:
- **Content model** (video, text, interactive, AI-generated, user-generated)
- **Learning methodology** (passive watching vs active practice vs project-based)
- **Gamification** (streaks, points, badges, leaderboards, currency)
- **AI features** (tutoring, personalization, assessment, content generation)
- **Mobile experience** (app quality, offline, PWA)
- **Community features** (forums, groups, social, mentorship)

### Phase 3 — Pricing Deep-Dive

Document:
- **Free tier** (what's included, limitations)
- **Paid tiers** (price points, what unlocks)
- **Enterprise/team pricing** (if available)
- **Certificates/credentials** (cost, recognition)
- **Price changes over time** (if known)

### Phase 4 — Brand & Messaging

Analyze:
- **Homepage hero message** (what's the first thing you see?)
- **Tagline / brand promise**
- **Visual identity** (colors, style, tone)
- **Social media presence** (follower counts, posting frequency, engagement)
- **App store listing** (description, screenshots, ratings, reviews)

### Phase 5 — SWOT

Build a SWOT matrix:
- **Strengths** — what do they do best?
- **Weaknesses** — where are they vulnerable?
- **Opportunities** — what could they do that they haven't?
- **Threats** — what market forces could hurt them?

### Phase 6 — Differentiation Map

Compare to our platform:
- Where do we directly compete?
- Where are we uniquely differentiated?
- What can we learn from their approach?
- What gaps in their offering can we exploit?

Present findings as a structured brief with a "So what?" section of actionable takeaways.
