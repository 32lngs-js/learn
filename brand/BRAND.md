# Palestra Brand Guide

## Brand Foundation

- **Name:** Palestra (ancient Greek/Roman training ground for practice)
- **Essence:** Earned Mastery
- **Personality:** Sharp, Composed, Demanding, Curious, Direct
- **Positioning:** For self-directed learners who refuse to settle for knowledge that fades — an AI-native platform that builds skills you actually retain.
- **Competitive wedge:** "We teach you to perform, not just understand."

## Logo

The Palestra mark is a bold geometric **P** with an indigo upward-pointing chevron that **breaks above the bowl boundary**, symbolizing upward mastery and breaking through limits.

- **Primary mark:** P monogram + "PALESTRA" wordmark (`v5-final.svg`)
- **Favicon/icon:** P monogram only (`favicon.svg`)
- **Light mode:** Dark mark (#18181B) on light background
- **Dark mode:** Light mark (#E8E8EC) on dark background, indigo chevron unchanged

## Colors

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary (dark) | `#18181B` | Text, logo on light backgrounds |
| Primary (light) | `#E8E8EC` | Text, logo on dark backgrounds |
| Accent (indigo) | `#4F46E5` | Chevron, CTAs, interactive elements, brand accent |
| Background (light) | `#FAFAFA` | Light mode surfaces |
| Background (dark) | `#0D0D14` | Dark mode surfaces |

## Typography

- **Headings:** Inter Bold (700)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (400) / Medium (500)
- **Monospace:** JetBrains Mono (for code)
- **Letter-spacing:** +4px on logo wordmark, standard elsewhere

## Voice & Tone

- **Clear** — Short sentences. No jargon.
- **Confident** — States facts. Doesn't hedge.
- **Respectful** — Treats users as capable adults.
- **Precise** — Says exactly what it means.

### Words we use vs avoid

| Use | Avoid |
|-----|-------|
| Master, retain, perform, drill, recall | Unlock, journey, empower, level up |
| Practice, session, review | Content, module (in user-facing copy) |
| Sharp, precise, clear | Fun, exciting, amazing |
| Earned, built, demonstrated | Achieved, conquered, crushed it |

## Messaging Pillars

1. **Active Recall, Not Passive Replay** — Every session makes you produce answers, not consume content.
2. **Skills That Transfer** — We train for performance in real contexts, not abstract comprehension.
3. **Retention You Can Measure** — Your progress is tracked, quantified, and real.
4. **Learn What Fascinates You** — Career skills and intellectual pursuits treated with equal rigor.
